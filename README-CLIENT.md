# Client Guide — Your AEO Real Estate Website

Welcome to your website! This guide explains what you can safely change and how to get the most out of your site.

---

## ✅ What You CAN Safely Change

### Using the Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
Your site includes a **Client Dashboard** where you can safely edit:
- **Agent Identity**: Your name, phone, email, brokerage, team name
- **Social Media Links**: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, X, TikTok, Google Business Profile
- **Page Content**: About page bio, Contact page introduction text

All changes made through the dashboard are safe — they only update your data, never the site's code.

### Using Visual Edits (Free!)
Lovable's **Visual Edits** feature lets you click on any text, color, or font on your site and change it directly — **no credits consumed**. This is the fastest and safest way to make cosmetic changes.

1. Click the **Edit** button in the bottom-left of your editor
2. Click on any element you want to change
3. Edit text, colors, or fonts directly
4. Click **Save** to apply

### Safe AI Prompt Examples
- "Change the hero headline to [your text]"
- "Update the phone number to [your number]"
- "Change the primary color to [your color]"
- "Replace the hero image with this uploaded photo"
- "Update the testimonials section with these reviews"

---

## ⚠️ What You Should NOT Touch

The following are core engine components that power your SEO, AEO pages, and data systems. Modifying them can break critical functionality:

- **Admin tools** (`/admin` page) — These are for your provider's use
- **AEO page engine** — The system that generates your SEO-optimized Q&A pages
- **Edge functions** — Backend logic for sitemaps, content generation, and backups
- **Routing structure** — How pages are organized and linked

### Prompts to AVOID
- ❌ "Redesign the admin panel"
- ❌ "Change how pages are generated"
- ❌ "Modify the sitemap"
- ❌ "Update the routing structure"
- ❌ "Change the database schema"

---

## 🔄 How to Revert Changes

If something goes wrong:

1. **Version History**: Every AI edit creates a restore point. Look in your chat history and click on any previous message to restore that version.
2. **Backups**: Your site data is automatically backed up every 24 hours. Go to `/admin` → Backups tab to restore a previous snapshot.

---

## 📞 Need Help?

If you're unsure about a change, ask your provider first. They can make complex edits safely or guide you through the right approach.
